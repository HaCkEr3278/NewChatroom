Ramen Nightmare

Game Concept

Welcome to *Ramen Nightmare*. Set in a haunted ramen shop, you must survive your night shift while dealing with cursed kitchen utensils and uncovering the dark history behind the restaurant. Will you make it through the night, or will you be consumed by the spirits that haunt the shop?

Game Instructions

In this survival horror game, you must manage your tasks in the ramen shop while dealing with haunted kitchen tools. Here are the rules and mechanics:

  • Survival Gameplay: Complete your tasks before the haunted objects get you.
  • Haunted Utensils: Objects like a rolling pin, broth spoon, and noodle box come to life. Beware of their movements!
  • Security Tools: Use cameras and locks to protect yourself. But be careful, resources are limited!
  • Resource Management: Balance time between preparing ramen and avoiding possessed objects.
  • Survive the Night: Your goal is to last through the hours until morning comes.

Prepare for unsettling noises, strange movements, and creepy encounters...
